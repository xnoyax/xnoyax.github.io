    I am a technologist, writer and consultant based in Copenhagen, Denmark.<br><br>
    I'm currently a PhD fellow at  <a href="https://www.cbs.dk/en/research/department-organisation/organizations-markets-and-governance-omg">Copenhagen Business School</a>, where I write about the political economy of distributed knowledge infrastructures and their epistemic consequences. <br><br>
    
    
    
    Previous projects:<br><br>

    <a href="https://unnamed.fund/">Unnamed Fund</a>, a cooperatively controlled, decentralized experimental fund with real-life outcomes, that seeks to re-imagine existing models for funding creative, artistic, and entrepreneurial projects. Unnamed Fund sustains those underserved by traditional funding through the equitable distribution of capital, across multiple impact outcomes.<br><br>

    <a href="https://brown.columbia.edu/portfolio/wolf-pack-how-media-coverage-of-criminal-justice-enabled-mass-incarceration/">Wolf Pack</a>, a research project funded by the <a href="https://brown.columbia.edu/">Brown Institute for Media Innovation</a> that investigates media influence on mass incarceration through a searchable database collecting 25 years of crime and criminal justice coverage.<br><br>

    <a href="/lineage">LINEAGE</a>, an AI visual search and discovery engine that makes image collections accessible and explorable using unsupervised machine learning.<br>
    Explore an experimental implementation of the LINEAGE engine on the Art Gallery of Greater Victoria digital collection <a href="/aggv">here</a>.<br><br>

    In 2023 I was studio researcher at the <a href="https://antikythera.xyz/">Antikythera</a> program at the Berggruen Institute, where I contributed to the <a href="https://cloudmegaregions.site">Cloud MegaRegionalization</a> project.<br><br>

    I am an alumna of and former community mentor at <a href="https://www.newinc.org/year-6-members/noya-kohavi">NEW INC</a>, the New Museum incubator at the intersection of art, design and technology.<br><br>
